About NishuAbhyasi 2.0
A free mock test platform — built with love

Hi! I'm Yashwant Singh, a Software Engineer by profession and a builder by passion.

This platform was originally built for my wife's Bihar B.Ed CET exam preparation — because I couldn't find any free, clean, and reliable mock test tool online without hidden paywalls or cluttered ads.

After seeing how much it helped her, and realising that thousands of students across Bihar face the same challenge, I decided to open this platform to everyone — completely free of cost.

NishuAbhyasi 2.0 runs on a fully cloud-based infrastructure with real-time question banks, adaptive scoring, and detailed analytics — all without charging students a single rupee. My goal is to keep it that way, forever, for as long as I can.

No ads. No hidden fees. No data selling. Just honest preparation. 🙏

Privacy Policy
We respect your data — always
🛡️
Your Passwords Are SafeAll account passwords are stored as industry-standard cryptographic hashes (SHA-256 + Firebase Auth). Not even the admin can see or access your password — ever. Only you hold that key.
📊
Test Data Stays YoursYour score history and attempt records are stored solely to help you track your own progress. The admin can view aggregate statistics to improve the question bank, but your personal details are treated with full discretion.
🍪
No Cookies or TrackingWe do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts from third parties. No browsing history, IP addresses, or behavioural data is collected or stored.
📧
We Never Sell Your DataYour email address, name, and any personal information you provide will never be sold, rented, or shared with any third party, advertiser, or commercial entity. Period.
🌐
InfrastructureThis platform is powered by Google Firebase (Firestore + Authentication), hosted in secure cloud data centres. All data transfers are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.
🙏
Our PromiseNishuAbhyasi is a student-first platform. It exists to help, not to monetise. We are committed to keeping it free, clean, and private — always.
